Colorchoir is a web audio instrument designed for massively multi-channel collaborative performance. It's for making noise on a whole lot of smartphones all at once. Current choir members: 10. High score (most ever members): 100. Brought to you by Morgan.

You might be wondering how this thing works, or where you should click, etc. The sounds are simpler to the left, and lower in pitch toward the top. So starting off at the top left is a nice way to start. You also might be wondering how multiple instances are synchronized. They talk to a server to establish universal notion of time, and work from there. You can see the source code on github if you like. Send me an email or a twitter if you have questions or whatnot.
